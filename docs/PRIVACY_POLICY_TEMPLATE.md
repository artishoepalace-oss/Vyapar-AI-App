# Privacy Policy Template - Vyapar AI

Vyapar AI helps small retailers track sales, profit, stock and business analytics.

Data collected:
- Account information for login
- Business profile entered by the user
- Sales, stock and profit data entered/uploaded by the user
- Files selected by the user for AI extraction
- Subscription status and purchase information

Data use:
- To provide business analytics and reports
- To extract structured sales/profit data from uploaded files
- To manage subscription access
- To backup and restore user data

Important:
- Personal identifiers from uploaded bills should be ignored by AI extraction when not required for business analytics.
- API keys are stored on backend servers, not inside the app.
- Users can export/delete their data from settings.

Replace this template with a lawyer-reviewed privacy policy before publishing.
